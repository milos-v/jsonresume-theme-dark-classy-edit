About

Started as a designer because being a designer gives you a chance to express yourself creatively. One time a website had to be finished, the design was done but no one in the company knew how to do HTML/CSS. I decided to give it a try. Somehow it came naturally to me and the more I learned the clearer it became to me that coding is also highly creative. It quickly captured the whole of my attention. Still for a long time design remained in my field of work, so I was able to constantly mix the two but still, gradually I switched to coding. In the past two years I've been doing mostly back-end programming and designing only as a hobby. I am eager to learn more and continue to improve my skills from project to project so I can have a bigger impact wherever I land.
